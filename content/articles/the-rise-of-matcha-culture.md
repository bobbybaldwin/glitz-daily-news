## FAQ


**Q:** What is matcha?

**A:** Matcha is a type of green tea that's finely ground and powdered, typically used to make a traditional Japanese tea ceremony drink.


**Q:** What are the health benefits of matcha?

**A:** Matcha is rich in antioxidants, contains high levels of L-theanine, and may help reduce stress and improve focus.


**Q:** How do I prepare matcha?

**A:** Matcha is prepared by whisking the green tea powder in hot water, creating a frothy and energizing drink. You can also add milk, sugar, or other ingredients to create a matcha latte or smoothie.
